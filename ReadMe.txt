Welcome to GrafftiCMS

As of December 11, 2009 - 

Before you can compile GraffitiCMS, you will first need to download a couple extra .dll's from http://graffiticms.com and place them in your sites lib folder.

To compile:

1. Copy the dll's from the GraffitiCMS.com site to your lib folder.
2. Open the GraffiitCMS.sln file. 
3. Click cntrol+shift+B.
